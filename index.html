after retirement

Now Jiayi Zhou leads a peaceful life with her beloved cat. She enjoys capturing beautiful things happening around her by camera, and maybe sometimes, she will casually write these little moments down.

After many years' chasing after the so-called self-fulfillment she finally realizes that what she really wants is the sensibility to feel, the freedom to create, and a little time that belongs to herself.

Btw, she's also a master in playing Guan Dan（掼蛋）, a famous Chinese poker game.
